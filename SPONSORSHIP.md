## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Will PermissionPilot always be free?**  
A: Yes. The open-source project will always be free. Sponsorship enables continued development and maintenance.

**Q: Can I get a refund?**  
A: GitHub Sponsors operates month-to-month. You can cancel anytime.

**Q: What if I sponsor but later want to stop?**  
A: No problem! You can change your tier or cancel at any time. Thank you for supporting us while you did.

**Q: Is sponsorship tax-deductible?**  
A: GitHub Sponsors works through GitHub. Consult your tax professional about deductibility in your jurisdiction.

**Q: Can companies sponsor?**  
A: Absolutely! Corporate sponsorship is welcome and appreciated.

**Q: Will sponsors get features non-sponsors don't?**  
A: No. We ship the same open-source code for everyone. Sponsors get early access to releases and closer communication, but no exclusive features.
